PANDA

Pandas are large, bear-like mammals native to China. They are known for their distinctive black and white fur and their gentle demeanor. Pandas primarily eat bamboo, which makes up 99% of their diet.

They are solitary animals, spending most of their time alone in the wild. Pandas are also known for their playful behavior, often climbing trees and rolling around on the ground.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND THEM
