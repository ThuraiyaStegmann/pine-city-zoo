ANIMALS MAP PLACES

KOALA

Koalas are marsupials native to Australia. They are known for their distinctive appearance, with large ears and a stout body. Koalas primarily eat eucalyptus leaves, which are toxic to most animals.

They are mostly nocturnal and spend most of their time sleeping in trees. Koalas are also known for their unique vocalizations, which can be heard during mating season.
