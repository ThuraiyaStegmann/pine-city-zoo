ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Experience the thrill of the Monkey Trail, a winding path that takes you through the treetops where you can observe various species of monkeys in their natural habitat. This immersive trail offers a unique perspective on these playful primates as they swing and play above you.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Monkey Trail located in the eastern section of the zoo, accessible from the main walking path near the reptile house.
